Primer Group of Companies

DC SM Pampanga Branch

Primer Group of Companies is one of the Philippines' leading retail and distribution conglomerates, known for bringing world-class lifestyle, sports, and outdoor brands to Filipino consumers. With a diverse portfolio of retail stores and distribution channels, Primer continues to grow as a trusted partner for both global brands and local customers across the country.

The DC SM Pampanga branch serves as a key distribution center in Central Luzon, supporting the retail operations of various Primer brands in the region and ensuring efficient product flow to meet customer demand.
